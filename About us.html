Who We Are

Techloop Solutions is a passionate group of young innovators driven by a shared vision: to empower rural communities through technology.

Based in Murang’a County, we bring together expertise in IT, education, and entrepreneurship to make digital access easy, affordable, and impactful across rural Kenya.

  • A diverse team of developers, trainers, and local advocates
  • Rooted in Murang’a, serving Embu, Nyeri, and surrounding villages
  • Committed to sustainable, scalable impact through innovation

What We Do

We deliver three pillars of digital empowerment to uplift rural communities:

Internet Access

Affordable hotspot internet services that connect homes, schools, and businesses in areas with limited or no connectivity.

Digital Services

CV writing, LinkedIn setup, email training, and social media management to help individuals and small businesses go digital.

Software Solutions

Custom websites, business systems, and tools tailored to the needs of rural entrepreneurs and community organizations.

Why We Exist

In many parts of rural Kenya, lack of digital access means missed opportunities — for jobs, education, and economic growth. We believe everyone deserves the power of the digital world at their fingertips.
