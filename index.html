About Techloop Solutions

At Techloop Solutions, we are on a mission to digitally empower rural communities across Kenya. Founded on the belief that technology access should be universal, we provide essential digital services that connect the unconnected and equip individuals with the skills needed in today's digital economy.

Our vision is to create a digitally inclusive society where location is not a barrier to opportunity. Through reliable internet services, accessible digital solutions, and practical tech education, we're helping bridge the urban-rural digital divide.

Our Mission

To democratize digital access and opportunities in underserved rural communities.

Our Vision

A Kenya where geographical location doesn't limit digital opportunities.
